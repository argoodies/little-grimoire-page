怎么玩

  • 拖动令牌/纽扣:按住任意令牌或纽扣拖到板面上想要的位置,松手它会自然歪斜落下,彼此不会重叠。
  • 旋转板子:拖动板面或空白背景即可旋转、翻面;翻到背面还有一副布局。
  • 缩放:双指捏合缩放(触屏),桌面可用鼠标滚轮或触控板。
  • 日夜:点左上角 ☀️/🌙 在暖光与冷光之间切换。
  • 重新开始:点右上角刷新按钮,重新随机摆放并播放开场翻转。

How to play

  • Drag tokens / buttons: press and drag any token or button onto the board; on release it settles at a slight angle, and pieces never overlap.
  • Rotate the board: drag the board surface or empty background to spin and flip it; the back has a second layout.
  • Zoom: pinch to zoom on touch; use the mouse wheel or trackpad on desktop.
  • Day / night: tap the ☀️/🌙 button (top-left) to switch warm and cool lighting.
  • Restart: tap the refresh button (top-right) to re-randomize the layout and replay the intro flip.

FAQ

Do I need an account? No — no sign-up or login, just open and play.

Do you collect my data? No. See the Privacy Policy.

Which platforms? Any modern browser (mobile/desktop); the iOS app is in TestFlight beta.

No sound? Mobile browsers require one tap/touch before audio starts — that's a browser policy.
