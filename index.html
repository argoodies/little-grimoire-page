简介About

小魔典把说书人的「魔典」搬上屏幕：一块可旋转、翻面的紫绒板，上面是可拖动的令牌与小纽扣。在推理与社交博弈类桌游中用它记录身份、提醒状态，全程在你的设备上运行。 A digital version of the storyteller's grimoire: a rotatable, flippable velvet board with draggable tokens and small buttons. Track roles and status reminders during social-deduction tabletop games — all on your own device.

  • 拖拽令牌Draggable pieces — 令牌与纽扣随手摆放，互不重叠。tokens and buttons you place freely; they never overlap.
  • 旋转翻面Rotate & flip — 拖动空白旋转板子，背面还有一副。drag empty space to spin; a second layout on the back.
  • 日夜切换Day / night — 暖光与冷光一键切换。warm or cool lighting in one tap.
  • 随处可玩Anywhere — 浏览器即开，也有 iOS 版本（TestFlight 内测）。runs in a browser; iOS app in TestFlight beta.
