这是什么What is it

小魔典把说书人的「魔典」搬上屏幕：一块可自由旋转、翻面的紫绒木板，上面是可拖动的纸板令牌与小纽扣。用它在推理与社交博弈类桌游中记录身份、提醒状态，全程在你的设备上运行。 Little Grimoire brings the storyteller's grimoire to your screen: a freely rotating, flippable purple-velvet board holding draggable cardboard tokens and small buttons. Use it to track roles and status reminders during social-deduction tabletop games — all running entirely on your device.

🎴

拖拽令牌Draggable tokens

纸板令牌与小纽扣，随手摆放、互不重叠。Cardboard tokens and buttons you drag into place; they never overlap.

🔄

旋转与翻面Spin & flip

拖动空白旋转板子，翻到背面还有一副。Drag empty space to rotate; flip to a second layout on the back.

🌗

日夜切换Day / night

一键在暖光与冷光之间切换气氛。Toggle between warm and cool lighting in one tap.
