隐私政策

生效日期:2026 年 7 月 9 日

小魔典(Little Grimoire)不收集、不存储、不分享任何个人数据。本 app 完全在你的设备本地运行,用于桌游辅助。

我们收集的数据

无。app 不要求注册或登录,没有账号系统,不收集姓名、邮箱、位置、通讯录、设备标识或使用行为。

本地数据

你的摆放布局、日夜等偏好(如有)仅保存在你自己的设备本地,不会上传到任何服务器。卸载或清除数据即可删除。

网络与第三方

app 本体不含广告、不含第三方分析或追踪 SDK,不会主动向我们或任何第三方发送任何数据。

儿童

本 app 不面向儿童收集任何信息,也不收集任何人的信息。

变更

如政策有更新,我们会更新本页顶部的生效日期。

联系

有任何疑问请联系:argoodies@googlegroups.com

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

Little Grimoire does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device as a tabletop-game aid.

Data we collect

None. The app has no sign-up or login, no accounts, and does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, device identifiers, or usage behavior.

Local data

Any preferences (such as your layout or day/night setting) are stored only locally on your own device and are never uploaded to any server. Uninstalling or clearing data removes them.

Network & third parties

The app contains no ads and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and sends no data to us or any third party.

Children

The app collects no information from children — or from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
